Recent Infrastructural Development

The University is extremely keen on betterment of infrastructure to promote a more conducive academic environment. Major developmental works taken up during the 2008-2013 period:

At the Main Campus:

  • Reconstruction of Gandhi Bhavan auditorium
  • Composite Building for the Science Departments
  • Composite Building for the Arts and Social Science Departments
  • Teachers' Quarter
  • Two floors of Rabindra Bhavan
  • Officers' Quarter
  • Thorough restructuring of Open Air Theatre
  • Three floors of Subarna Jayanti Bhavan
  • Toilet and ramp in sixteen buildings for physically challenged persons
  • International Guest House at Padmapukur
  • Sports Centre(work started)
  • Technology Bhavan (under construction)
  • Electrical revamping (work started)
  • Construction of drinking water tanks



At the Saltlake Campus:

  • Guest House for the Academic Staff College
  • Artificial Rock
  • Auditorium (work started)



At the West Annexe (NIL) Campus:

  • Thorough renovation of two portions of the existing building
  • Thorough renovation of sewer and drainage system
